Shree Swami Samarth

"The great spiritual master from Akkalkot"

About Swami Samarth

Swami Samarth Maharaj, also known as Akkalkot Swami, was a 19th-century Indian spiritual master believed to be an incarnation of Lord Dattatreya. They lived in Akkalkot, Maharashtra, and guided countless devotees with there teachings, blessings, and miraculous powers.

Qualities & Teachings

  • Compassionate to all living beings
  • Promoter of self-realization and devotion
  • Taught the power of faith and surrender
  • Helped people overcome problems through spiritual guidance

Famous Quotes

  • "Bheevu nakos, mee tujhyapathishee aahe!" (Do not fear, I am with you!)
  • "Jevha jevha sankat yel, majha naam smaran kar!" (Whenever trouble arises, remember me!)
